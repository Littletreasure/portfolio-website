Hello, I am Ruth. I am currently studying at Northcoders.

I was born in Sheffield and moved to Southport with my family in 1993.

I worked for Sefton Council for 25 years in 3 different roles. Firstly as a Technical Clerk in the Environmental Protection Department. Secondly as a Data Analyst in the School Organisation Section of Children's Services. Thirdly as a Researcher for the Early Help and Children's Social Care Teams.
