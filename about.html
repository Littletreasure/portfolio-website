About Me

Hello, I am Ruth. I was born in Sheffield and moved to Southport with my family in 1993.

I worked for Sefton Council for 25 years in 3 different roles. Firstly as a Technical Clerk in the Environmental Protection Department. Secondly as a Data Analyst in the School Organisation Section of Children's Services. Thirdly as a Researcher for the Early Help and Children's Social Care Teams. I was made redundant in January 2020. Following a conversation with a friend who was attending Northcoders, I made the decision to enrol and pursue a new career.

In my spare time I enjoy running and have completed a couple of half marathons and a metric marathon. I am a member of Southport Waterloo Athletics Club and I attend parkrun regularly.
